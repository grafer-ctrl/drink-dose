Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Last updated: November 8, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collection

Drink-Dose does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

We never ask for your name, email, or any identifying information.

2. Local Processing Only

All water intake calculations and hydration tracking happen entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

  • Your inputs (weight, age, activity) are processed instantly
  • Results are displayed immediately in your browser
  • No cookies, no tracking, no server logs

3. Browser Notifications (Optional)

You may enable hourly hydration reminders via browser notifications. These are managed by your browser and do not involve our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display non-personalized ads. These ads are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not share any user data with advertisers.

5. Analytics (Anonymous)

We may use anonymized analytics to improve performance. No personal data is collected.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.

8. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at contact@hydrolife.com
